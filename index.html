MOTIVATION FACTOR TOWARDS ONLINE EDUCATION

MY LIFE GOAL IS TO: Get a better result and score in upcoming exam even during MCO

Factors of Motivation:

1. Achievement

2. Recognition

3. The work itself

4. Responsibility


1. Achievement - It will provide a proud feeling of having done something difficult but worthwhile.

2. Recognition - This recognition should come from both their superiors and their peers.

3. The work itself - The job or job course itself must be interesting, varied, and provide enough of a challenge to keep students motivated.

4. Responsibility - Students should “own” their work. They should hold themselves responsible for this completion and not feel as though they are being micromanaged.
